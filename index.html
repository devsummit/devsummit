What is DevSummit

Indonesia Developer Summit is an event specifically aimed to stakeholders of the emerging technology industry in Indonesia, and in general, for the public who are the users of it's services, products, and services.

The event is open to public, packed in a 3 days conference that consists of three major topics: Artificial Intelligence, BlockChain and Open Source.
